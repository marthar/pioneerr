About

Pioneer Reality Studio creates engaging experiences using cutting-edge technologies. We put an emphasis on concept development, experimentation, and iterative practices. While exploring the potential of existing technologies, we keep the future in sight, on the lookout for upcoming technical advances.

Sofie Hodara

Sofie Hodara is a multimedia artist, designer, and educator. Her work explores the intersection between traditional and cutting-edge media in order to create beautiful, non-utilitarian experiences with technology. She is a member artist at Bromfield Gallery and frequent collaborator with colleague and friend, Martha Rettig. She teaches design at Massachusetts College of Art and Design, SMFA at Tufts University, and Emmanuel College.

Martha Rettig

Martha Rettig is a designer, experimenter, and immersive artist whose work focuses on merging traditional mediums with emerging technologies. Her experience over the past fifteen years crosses many disciplines, including design concept, visual design, interactive design, interface design, data visualization, experience design, and creative direction. She co-founded an interactive design agency, Cykod, in 2006 and helped build over two hundred websites, and apps from the ground up. Martha currently is an Assistant Professor at Massachusetts College of Art + Design teaching in the undergraduate Graphic Design department and the coordinator of the Dynamic Media Institute MFA program.

our work

We are currently developing tools and curriculum to aid in teaching and implementing emerging technologies for artists and designers. Alongside our work, we have student projects developed for or in conjunction with our classes.

CabinAR

CabinAR is an free, web-based application that allows you to make and share marker-based augmented reality. Create an account to use the online editor and build original content.

Disobedient Design Exhibition

The final exhibition of MassArt's Spring 2019 interdisciplinary elective Disobedient Design; It featured AR letterpress posters made by each student.

Rethink Recycle

An augmented reality workshop and letterpress exhibition organized by students of MassArt's Spring 2019 interdisciplinary elective Disobedient Design.

The Pursuit of Happiness

An augmented reality poster exhibition featuring over 200 years of letterpressed presidential campaign slogans (Bromfield Gallery, October 2018).

Ghost Memorials by Ceaser Duarte

Duarte concepted and prototyped Ghost Memorials in A/VR for our Storytellers and Designers course. He installed marker-based AR interventions at ghost bikes in Cambridge MA. The AR concet celebrated the lives of the deceased.

Boston, Aquarium! by Cailgh MacDonald

A custom augmented reality game designed for the Boston Aquiarium.

Selfie

Interactive augmented polaroids designed for Self-Composure exhibition (Avairy Gallery, June 2018). Using custom markers, they prompt cell-phone using participants to record a selfie video that they can then watch in the polaroid.
